1. **Traffic Split**: 50/50 between Version A and B
2. **Sample Size**: Minimum 2,000 visitors per variation
3. **Test Duration**: 14 days minimum
4. **Secondary Metrics**: Email signups, demo requests, trial starts
5. **Segmentation**: By industry, business size, traffic source
